Ertugul's Cavalry

Artist : Fausto Zonaro
Year: 1854-1929
Starting Price: $4,300,000

This painting is of the Ertugrul Cavalry Regiment crossing the Galata Bridge. During the reign of Suleiman the Magnificent as well as the 16th and 17th centuries, the Ottoman Empire's power and prestige was at its greatest. The Ottoman uniform for 40 years had a dark blue coat, pants and tarbush.

Self Portrait 1889

Artist: Vincent van Gogh
Year: 1889
Starting Price: $2,300,000

In 1889, Vincent Van Gogh painted his last self portrait in oil canvas. It is said to have been painted in Saint-Rémy-de-Provence in southern France.

Café Terrace at Night

Artist: Vincent van Gogh
Date: 1888
Starting Price: $13,00,000

Vincent Van Gogh painted Café Terrace at Night in Arles, being one of the first nighttime scenes created. The painting causes much confusion as to what the meaning of the painting is.

Mona Lisa

Artist: Leonardo da Vinci
Year: 1503
Starting Price: $22,000,000

Mona Lisa, also known as the Portrait of Lisa Gherardini, wife of Francesco del Giocondo, Italian La Gioconda, or French La Joconde. It was an oil painting on a poplar wood panel created by Leonardo da Vinci when he was living in Florence. Most possibly the world’s most famous painting. It was said to have been painted between 1503 and 1519.

The Card Players

Artist: Paul Cézanne
Year: 1894-1895
Starting Price: $6,200,000

Painted by the French Post-Impressionist Paul Cézanne, The Card Players is one of the five of a series of oil paintings painted during the early 1890's in Cézanne'S final period.

Balloon Girl

Artist: Banksy
Year: 2002
Starting Price: $1,600,000

Girl with Balloon is a 2002-started London series of stencil murals by the graffiti artist Banksy, depicting a young girl with her hand extended toward a red heart-shaped balloon carried away by the wind.
